Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

CourtVision is a badminton coaching app that provides match recording, live streaming, and scorekeeping. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Data We Access

When you connect your YouTube account, CourtVision accesses:

  • Your YouTube live streaming capabilities (to create and manage live broadcasts)
  • Your scheduled live broadcasts (to list upcoming broadcasts you can stream to)

How We Use Your Data

  • YouTube access is used solely to create, manage, and end live broadcasts on your behalf during matches
  • Match data (scores, events) is stored locally on your device
  • Player names are stored locally on your device

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. YouTube authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's keychain.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. The only external communication is between your device and YouTube's API servers when you use the live streaming feature.

Data Deletion

You can revoke CourtVision's access to your YouTube account at any time through your Google Account settings. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

CourtVision uses the YouTube Data API v3. Your use of YouTube features is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: courtvisionlab@gmail.com
